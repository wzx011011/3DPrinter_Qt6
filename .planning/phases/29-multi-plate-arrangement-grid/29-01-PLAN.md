## Naming convention

The Qt6 codebase (per CONVENTIONS.md) uses **camelCase methods** (e.g. `plateCount()`, `setCurrentPlateIndex()`, `objectIndicesOnPlate()`). The geometry methods therefore use camelCase to match the surrounding file: `plateStrideX()`, `plateStrideY()`, `computeShapePosition()`, `computeOrigin()`, `computePlateIndex()`, `updatePlateCols()`, `updatePlateOrigins()`, `setPlateSize()`. The single exception is the **file-scope free function** `compute_colum_count(int)` — kept snake_case to mirror upstream `PartPlate.hpp:38-50` exactly (it is NOT a member). Member FIELDS use the `m_` snake_case prefix (m_plate_cols, m_plate_width, etc.) matching upstream and the existing m_plate_list/m_current_plate.
