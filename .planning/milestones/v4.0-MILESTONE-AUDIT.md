- **Preview shell flow:** `BBLTopbar.qml`, `main.qml`, and `Plater.qml` keep
  Prepare/Preview in the shared Plater container while workflow tabs dispatch
  through `BackendContext::requestSelectTab`.
- **Preview renderer flow:** `PreviewPage.qml` binds `GLViewport` to
  `PreviewViewModel` payload, layer, move, travel, role visibility, marker, and
  color-mode state.
- **Layer/move flow:** `PreviewLayerRail.qml` and `MoveSlider.qml` route all
  user-visible controls through Q_INVOKABLE ViewModel APIs.
- **Role/color flow:** `PreviewViewModel` owns upstream-mapped role colors,
  view-mode ordering, availability state, legend data, and dense renderer masks.
- **Verification flow:** `QmlUiAuditTests` now guards restored Preview resources,
  stale path absence, workflow tab operability, placeholder cleanup, and
  renderer-safe Preview controls.
